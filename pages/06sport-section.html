Bristol to Bath 25K

If you’re looking for an opportunity to test your mettle and raise some cash for the hospital charity in the process, you could consider signing up for the Bristol to Bath 25K Trail Challenge. From the Bristol and Bath Science Park to the Royal Victoria Park in Bath, you can marvel in the beautiful scenery all while helping elicit donations for the hospital charity. This will be the second event and entries are filling in fast. The race will be held on Saturday 27th of September so don’t delay your entry submission.

Anyone interested should contact Community Fundraising Manager Hannah Jones for more information and discounted entry.
