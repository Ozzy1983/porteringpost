❖Porterscope

The Crossword: Why not take a break from the cryptic crosswords, treat yourself to a Metro Easy Sudoku? No point boiling your brain when the weather outside is hitting 30C+!

The Sleeper: The rising temperatures can lead to a susceptibility to snooze. Keep yourself awake, alert and motivated by checking the trays every 5 minutes.

The Timekeeper: The days are longer and there is a lazy haze to the atmosphere. Don’t let this muggy malaise hold you back, treat yourself to a new watch with an extra loud alarm so you don’t risk oversleeping.

The Digestive: Burgers by the beach and sausages on the sand, it might make for a great way to spend the weekends, but you’ll need to walk that excess weight off. Luckily there is a full tray of work for you to do.

The Seated: Sitting in the sun, enjoying a cool summer breeze feels so much more satisfying after a good day’s work smashing through the lists. Make the relaxation more fulfilling by grabbing that 8A before you go.

The Coffe Bearer: Iced coffees may be a great way to cool down, but if you really want to avoid becoming drenched in sweat, then maybe try rushing patients to MRI, the breeze and the AC will bring welcome relief.

The Sicknote: Sick of all the pool parties and barbecues? The best way to avoid the crowded beaches and city streets is to pick up some extra shifts and help bring those waiting lists down.

The Missing: Disappearing on a summer break may be some people’s idea of a great time, but you know better. Get your healthy, sunny glow by taking the empty oxygen bottles outside to the Oxy Store to be replaced.

The Backbone: As we near the middle of the year, the time has come to rest those weary bones. You can’t expect to prop up the team forever, so remember to take a little time for some self-care.

The Coin: Make hay while the sun is shining, or so the old adage goes. Now is the time to book all those bank shifts while others sun themselves on Weston-Super-Mare beach, then we’ll see who’s smiling come Christmas.

The Sandwich: Barbecue weather may have arrived, and it’s a great excuse to get the whole gang together, but an even better team building exercise is working together to empty the trays.

The Mariner: Summers spent by the beach, casting a line into the ocean, with hopes of catching the elusive Big One. Only 4 ITUs back-to-back could be more relaxing.
