Parking Plans in Action

In response to the ongoing parking problems faced by staff at the hospital there will now be a relaunch of the Travel Engagement Programme.

This will be a series of events such as workshops and feedback sessions, along with surveys to gain as much information from staff about their travel options to work and challenges they face in doing so. They will be looking to hear from as many people as they can to inform further changes and support staff with their commutes. They are also aiming to promote the use of more sustainable and greener options. Those staff with an existing permit will be able to retain this, however charges will begin to apply from October 2025. This is currently proposed to be 0.75% of your salary, this is approximately ¬£15 a month for someone on Band 2 working 37.5hrs a week. The first deduction will be from your payslip in October. If you would like to avoid the charges you must cancel your permit and should look to do so before the first payment date. As they are looking to base the plans on feedback from people employed at NBT, you should regularly check your work email for any updates or proposed amendments to any plans. You can also make your voice heard on this matter as well as put forward suggestions or concerns you may have. More information can be found on the Trust LINK pages and is updated regularly.
