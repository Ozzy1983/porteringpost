Cup Success for Unathletic

The first season of the Eurota League is now over and we celebrate the new cup champions Imaging Unathletic. As the first ever winners of the Horseman Cup they will receive a small gift each and the coveted Horseman Cup Trophy. Teams will now go through a reselection period and a chance for a whole new squad to rise through the ranks of next year’s league, beginning 16th August 2025.
Congratulations to Imaging Unathletic!

Transfer Window Trashed?

With the change in how the Trust is recruiting, most vacancies are now internal only. This is part of the plan to passively reduce the overall staffing numbers where possible, however it does mean that for the foreseeable future all of the changes to the line ups will come from existing staff in other parts of the Trust.
