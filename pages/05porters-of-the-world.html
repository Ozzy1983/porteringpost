Here we will delve into the wonderful world of International Porters, each edition focussing on a new country linked to our team. For this edition we will be shining a spotlight on: Republic of Korea

Hanguk 한국

The Repulic of Korea declared its independence in 1948 and became a fully independent nation in 1952 after the San Francisco Peace Treaty was signed with the Japanese. It was invaded by North Korea in 1950 which led to a brutal war that killed approximately 3 million Koreans and ended with a Demilitarised Zone being established between the two countries. Since then, South Korea has flourished and become a highly developed nation and a high-income economy, with a number of internationally renowned companies such as Samsung, Hyundai and LG.
