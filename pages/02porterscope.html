❖Porterscope

The Crossword

Autumn can be a time for reflection. Instead of your usual brain taxing cryptic crossword, how about you try a contemplative Autumn haiku this year? 5-7-2

The Sleeper

The days will soon be getting shorter, and these darker evenings will be messing with your circadian rhythms. The best way to stay alert and focussed is to keep on the go by grabbing the job tickets first.

The Timekeeper

Though there may be fewer hours of daylight soon, there are still the same number of hours of work. Make the most of your time by diligently ploughing on and helping to reduce those waiting lists.

The Coin

Things will begin to look a little lean soon, harvests not being as bountiful as they once were. Now is the time to consider longer-term, more reliable crops to sow.

The Digestive

The Summer is almost over and the time for relaxing with a nice cuppa and cheeky biscuit is soon to arrive. Don’t get too comfortable though, work is calling and there are plenty of jobs to get your teeth into.

The Seated

A warm and cosy seat by the fire may be calling you, but it will feel undeserved if you don’t feel that lovely tingling ache that only comes from a hard day’s work.

The Coffee Bearer

Pumpkin spice lattes are usually all the rage for the latter end of the year, but best drink for a Porter this autumn season is an energy drink. Get that sugar boost to help you to clear the lists each day.

The Sicknote

Hay fever season may be almost behind us, but flu season is swiftly approaching. Head on down to the Pharmacy soon to stock up on various medicines and avoid any extra absences.

The Missing

While the falling leaves may be a beautiful sight, don’t go getting too distracted and wander off outside. There are plenty of tickets of all colours falling gently into the tray for you.

The Backbone

You have remained upright and resolute all year so far, don’t give up now! As your colleagues’ energies wane, you can be the inspirational beacon that drives them ever onward.

The Sandwich

Hot soup and crusty bread may be the perfect comfort food as the days draw in, but why note sate your hunger for work by grabbing a handful of jobs and smashing through the backlogs.

The Mariner

Now is the time to make a last-ditch attempt at that one big catch. Soon the lakes will freeze over and opportunities will dry up, so keep your nose to the grindstone while you can.
