Transfer Window Trashed?

With the change in how the Trust is recruiting, most vacancies are now internal only. This is part of the plan to passively reduce the overall staffing numbers where possible, however it does mean that for the foreseeable future all of the changes to the line ups will come from existing staff in other parts of the Trust.
