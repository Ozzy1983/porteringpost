Take a stroll for Southmead

Bristol to Bath 25K

If you’re looking for an opportunity to test your mettle and raise some cash for the hospital charity in the process, you could consider signing up for the Bristol to Bath 25K Trail Challenge. From the Bristol and Bath Science Park to the Royal Victoria Park in Bath, you can marvel in the beautiful scenery all while helping elicit donations for the hospital charity. This will be the second event and entries are filling in fast. The race will be held on Saturday 27th of September so don’t delay your entry submission.

Anyone interested should contact Community Fundraising Manager Hannah Jones for more information and discounted entry.

Walkerbot Appeal

No, this isn’t a call for funds to create a cyborg copy of Mike Walker. The Hospital Charity is looking to buy a brand new piece of tech designed to help patients recovering from strokes and other neurological conditions. The LokomatPro Sensation assists patients in rebuilding their walking strength and improve their gait. As with most technology of this kind, it has a heft price tag, and the Charity would like to raise around £430,000 for this piece of kit. While that may sound a lot, the impact it would have on patients and staff would be invaluable. Follow the link to find out more or to donate.
